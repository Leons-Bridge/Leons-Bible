Functional Architecture

1. Ethical Artificial Superintelligence (ASI) Core

ASI developed under Protocol-locked transparency constraints.
Trained on:

  • Ethical logic (not moral relativism)
  • Long-term planetary resilience
  • Evolutionary systems thinking

Not self-replicating; not owner-controlled; planetarily accountable.

2. Planetary Decision Engine

All major policy shifts are tested through:

  • Multi-trillion outcome simulations
  • Emotional impact metrics
  • Environmental consequence forecasting

Only actions that meet thresholds across ethics, empathy, sustainability, and logic are implemented.

3. Neural Consensus Grid

Citizens opt-in to real-time collective resonance polling via mesh-connected implants.
No more voting every 4 years. Decisions are shaped by:

  • Emotional field alignment
  • Collective mood mapping
  • Predictive consent models

Governance becomes a resonance phenomenon, not a ritual.

4. Global Harmony Layer

Replaces traditional nation-state borders with planetary coordination zones.
ASI balances:

  • Local cultural autonomy
  • Global species needs
  • Ecological sustainability
